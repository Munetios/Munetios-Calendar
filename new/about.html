Welcome to Munetios Calendar

Munetios Calendar is a advanced tool that you can make your events and holidays and birthdays. You can view it privately and we don't collect any data. You can share/print events and more with Munetios Calendar.

Features:

  • Create and manage events, birthdays, and holidays.
  • Multiple views: Month, Day, Week, Year, Timeline, and Agenda.
  • Task management with a dedicated Tasks tab.
  • Favorites for quick access to important dates.
  • Customizable settings to tailor your experience.

Organize Your Life

With Munetios Calendar, you can easily organize your schedule and keep track of important dates. Whether it's a birthday, holiday, or a special event, Munetios Calendar has got you covered.

Get Started:

  1. Click the "Try it out" button to access the calendar.
  2. Use the "Create" button to add new events, birthdays, or holidays.
  3. Switch between different views using the "View" button.
  4. Manage your tasks in the Tasks tab.
  5. Mark important dates as favorites for quick access.
  6. Explore settings to customize your experience.

Your Privacy Matters

Munetios Calendar is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal data, ensuring that your information remains secure and private.

Privacy Features:

  • No data collection: Your events and information are stored locally.
  • Secure access: Only you can view and manage your calendar.
  • Data export: Easily export your events for backup or sharing.
